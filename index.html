About

Big Red Gifts is a campus wide gift exchange program that is open to all Cornell students. The goal of the program is to spread the joy of the holiday season and to promote community bonding through a fun activity. Sign up if you want to become a part of this new Cornell tradition.

How to Join

When you click the sign up button, you will be taken to a form where you can fill out your preferences on gift types and price ranges.

After everyone has been matched with a gift exchange partner, you will receive an email from us detailing who your partner is, how to send your gift to them, and how you're going to receive your gift.

The official gift exchange will take place the week after thanksgiving!
